About us

At Tiny Foot Studio, we specialize in maternity, newborn, and first birthday photography, creating heartwarming stories that you’ll cherish forever. With a team of skilled photographers and a Lakmé-trained makeup artist, we combine expertise and creativity to capture your journey with perfection. Whether it’s a cozy in-studio session at our Faridabad branch, an at-home shoot with themed setups, or a premium

experience at our Delhi and Noida collaboration studios, we ensure every detail is beautifully crafted. From props and gowns to cinematic videos and custom albums, we go beyond photography to deliver memories filled with love, joy, and elegance. Serving families across Delhi NCR, we take pride in transforming fleeting moments into treasures for generations to come.
